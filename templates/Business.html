We have over 30 years of experience in external talent management. Our vision of the labor and
skills market is that of a market undergoing profound transformation:

  • Record tensions on the executive market: the main challenge for large organizations is to
    find the right skills, beyond, and even before knowing, whether they should be employees,
    service providers or freelancers.
  • Wage employment increasingly challenged: more and more talents are expressing their
    desire to leave wage employment to experiment with other forms of employment.
  • Freelancing, an increasingly popular status: freelance status meets the new expectations of
    talents looking for meaning and freedom.
